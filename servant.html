Meet Denise!

I’m passionate about the Teaneck community and serving in our wonderful town.

🗹 Teaneck Resident for 35 years & Lifelong Resident of Bergen County

🗹 Planning Board Member Actively Present, Engaged and Involved in serving 2017-2019

🗹Founding Member Teaneck Road Business and Community Allance

🗹 Licensed Real Estate Broker and Educator of XCEED Realty and School of Real Estate Studies

🗹 Affordable Housing Forum organizer hosted for Teaneck Residents 2018-2019

🗹 Possess a Clear Understanding of Housing Stock, Development and Zoning

🗹 Economic Empowerment Chair of Coalition of 100 Black Women Bergen/Passaic Chapter

🗹Member of AA Chamber of Commerce of NJ

🗹Former President of Teaneck Chamber of Commerce

🗹Married for 26 years and mother to 2 Gorgeous Girls

🗹Minister at Christian International Church

My belief is that Diversity is a fundamental strength of Teaneck which includes diversity of ideas, time and talent which is equally important. It’s for this reason I’m running for Town Council and seek your support.
