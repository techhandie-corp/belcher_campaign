Denise Belcher for the Teaneck Council

On the 100th anniversary of the ratification of the 19th ammendment to the Constitution, which ensured the women's right to vote, we are reminded of the great sacrifices many have made for the right to vote.

Historically, the Women's Suffrage movement played a key role in the achievement of that milestone. Like the female leaders of that movement, Denise Belcher, is a strong steady voice for ALL of Teaneck, with a love for community and a love for service.
