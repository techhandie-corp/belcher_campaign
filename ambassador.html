Help Get the Vote Out

Dear Friends,

Development.Infrastructure.Taxes.Quality of life.

Teaneck has been grappling with these issues unsuccessfully for decades — but these challenging issues need to be addressed by careful planning and forward thinking.

That’s why I am running for town council.

It’s time for us to come together, as a community, to secure the future of Teaneck for all residents.

»
We need smart development that will allow our youth to learn and grow, our seniors to age in place, and our local businesses to thrive.
»
We need to invest wisely in our roads, public buildings, and open spaces to preserve and enhance the quality of life that drew us to this remarkable community
»
We need responsible, ethical governance that is informed by the public interest and guided by input from the extraordinary talents and expertise of this diverse community.

I hope I can count on you to contribute to my campaign, not only financially, but also by volunteering your time and talents — both now, during the campaign, and later, when I am on the town council—to help transform the council and get Teaneck back on track to true progress. I need your support to transform Teaneck with a 2020 Vision for change!

Please complete the attached donation/volunteer form and send it back as soon as you can. Together, we can transform Teaneck!
