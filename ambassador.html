Dear Friends,

The Block Ambassador program is a Get Out The Vote (GOTV) effort that assigns interested volunteers as "Block Captains" over their street to help us in:

»
Lawn Sign Placement
»
Cottage Parties
»
Voter Registration
»
Overall Voter Engagement

Interested? Please sign up here(check the box for Block Ambassador). It's GREAT that we have people who have already signed up. This is our Army of Volunteers. We need to reach out to them and nurture as soon as possible.
